*Last updated: 2026-02-25*

## Day One (dayoneapp.com)

**Category:** Personal Journaling App  
**Platforms:** iOS, Android, Mac, Apple Watch, Web  
**Pricing:** Free (limited) / Premium $49.99/year ($4.17/mo)

### What They Do Well

**1. "On This Day" Feature** ⭐
- Shows memories from past years on the same date
- Users LOVE this - creates daily engagement habit
- "The amount of times I pull out Day One to answer 'When did we...' is amazing"
- **YT Opportunity:** Our "Memory of the Day" could do this for families

**2. Frictionless Capture**
- Voice-to-text transcription
- Email-to-entry (forward emails to journal)
- Browser extensions (Safari/Chrome) to save webpages
- Auto-metadata: time, date, weather, step count, location
- **YT Opportunity:** Similar auto-capture for memories + photos

**3. Multi-Format Entries**
- Text, photos, videos, audio, drawings
- Unlimited media on premium
- PDF embedding and document scanning
- **YT Opportunity:** We have this, but voice recording UX could improve

**4. Journaling Streaks**
- Gamification that works - users report 1000+ day streaks
- Calendar view shows visual history
- SMS reminders that you can REPLY to directly
- **YT Opportunity:** Add streaks to our engagement system?

**5. Book Printing**
- Turn journals into physical books
- 25% discount for premium members
- **YT Opportunity:** We have Prodigi photobooks - could add journal printing

**6. Privacy-First Messaging**
- "Completely private digital space"
- End-to-end encryption
- Export options so data stays yours
- **YT Opportunity:** Already our strength, lean into it more

### Key Stats
- 150,000+ 5-star reviews
- Apple App of the Year winner
- Apple Design Award winner
- Users report 10+ years of daily journaling

### What YoursTruly Does Better

1. **Family/Legacy Focus** - Day One is individual, we're multi-generational
2. **Circles** - Shared memories with family members
3. **PostScripts** - Future messages (Day One doesn't have this)
4. **AI Clone** - Digital essence preservation
5. **Journalist Interviews** - Guided memory capture from others
6. **Engagement Gamification** - Our bubble system is more fun

### Features to Consider Adding

1. **"On This Day"** - Show memories from past years, huge engagement driver
2. **Journal Streaks** - Add to engagement XP system
3. **SMS Reply to Journal** - Reply to reminder = new entry
4. **Auto-metadata** - Weather, step count, location auto-added
5. **Browser Extension** - Clip articles to memories
6. **Calendar View** - Visual history of all entries
7. **Apple Watch App** - Quick voice capture on wrist

## Competitive Positioning

**Day One:** "Journal for your individual daily life"
**YoursTruly:** "Document your legacy for the people you love"

Day One is a *diary*. YoursTruly is a *legacy platform*.

They overlap in personal journaling but we differentiate with:
- Multi-generational focus (not just you)
- Future delivery (PostScripts)
- Digital preservation (AI clone)
- Family sharing (Circles)
- Guided capture (Journalist interviews)
